About Us

ActiveBeat Center, located in Dublin's vibrant Harold's Cross neighborhood, is a premier fitness facility offering a diverse range of classes and activities. From intense Crossfit workouts to the serenity of Yoga, the exhilarating rhythms of Salsa dance, the discipline of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, the fun of Zumba, the unique art of Capoeira, and the intensity of Thai Boxing, we have something for everyone. Our experienced instructors are dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness goals in a supportive and inclusive community. Come join us at ActiveBeat Center and experience the transformative power of movement and the joy of reaching new heights in your fitness journey.
